Target Audience

As the community already has members aged 35-50, the website will be targeted at a younger generation, while not deterring the older members.
This will be achieved by a simple layout (so as to not overwhelm the older audiance) and bright classic "Miami Beach, 1970" colours.
The main focus of the website is to draw in potential new players with a large sign up campaign, the secondary focus is to deliver news to current members.
This can be seen clearly on the home page but can also be said of the site in general,
with prompts to sign up through out body content and by promoting a fun, friendly lifestyle.
